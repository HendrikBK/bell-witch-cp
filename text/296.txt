Mr. Bell said he frequently went out to see what
was the matter, but could discover nothing. He

it might alarm them, and thinking too that it
was some one playing pranks, and by watching
he would be able to discover the intruder. Such
demonstrations continued to increase, being heard
two or three times during a week, and become so
intolerable that Mr. Bell determined to lay some
