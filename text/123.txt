This new development added to the sensation
already created. The news spread, and people
came in larger numbers, and the great anxiety
concerning the mystery prompted many ques-
tions in the effort to induce the witch to disclose
its own identity and purpose. Finally, in answer,
to the question, “Who are you and what do you
want?” the reply came, “I am aspirit; I was once
very happy but have been disturbed.” This was
uttered in a very feeble voice, but sufficiently
distinet to be understood by all present, and this
was all the information that could be elicited
for the time.
