Jushua Gardner was a very handsome young
man, graceful in appearance and cultured in
manners, and very entertaining socially. He was
of a good family, and had won the distinction of
being the sprightliest youth in school. Every
one conceded that Josh was a line fellow, who
would make his way in the world, and his atten-
tions to Betsy were not displeasing to the old
folks nor her brothers.
