The first marriage in the family was that of
Esther, who wedded Alex. Bennett Porter, July
24th, 1817, Rev. Thomas Gunn officiating at the
altar. Esther was a very prepossessing young
lady, gifted with many graces and charms which
made her attractive. Bennett Porter was also
popular, and the wedding was quite a noted
event. Jesse Bell, the eldest son, married Miss
Martha Gunn, daughter of Rev. Thomas Gunn.
This marriage took place several months later.
Both couples settled in the neighborhood, making
a fair start in life, sharing the confidence and
good will of thecommunity. A year or two after
the death of John Bell, Sr., the two families emi-
grated to Penola county, Miss., where they settled
for life and raised large and interesting families,
and have many descendants there at present.
