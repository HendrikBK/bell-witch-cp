Kate Gets in Bed With William Porter.

William Porter was a very prominent citizen
of the community, a gentleman of high integrity,
regarded for his strict veracity. He was also a
good friend to our family, and spent many nights
with us during the trouble, taking his turn with
others in entertaining Kate, which was necessary
to have any peace at all, and also agreeable to
those of an investigating turn of mind who were
not afraid, and this was Mr. Porter’s character;
like John Johnson, he rather cultivated the spirit,
and said he was fond of gabbing with Kate. This
seemed to please the witch, and they got along
on good terms. William Porter was at this time
