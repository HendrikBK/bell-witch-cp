Mrs. Ayers was asked if she was willing to tell
all she knew about the Bell Witch? “Oh no, I
could not tell the half I have heard in a week;
strictly speaking, I know nothing. I was bora
in the middle of the most exciting events, and
and they say that the witch was the first to carry
the news of my birth to the Bell family. All I
know is hearsay from father, mother, Grandfather
James Johnson, Uncle Calvin Johnson, Joel Bell,
and everybody who lived in the neighborhood at
that time, and, of course, I believe their statements
as firmly as if 1 had witnessed the demonstrations.”
