Kate made frequent visits to North Carolina,
John Bell’s old neighborhood, never absent
longer than a day or an hour, but always report-

and all members of the family except Mrs. Lucy
Bell, to whom the witch was devoted, declaring
that “Old Luce” was a good woman, but mani-

Bell, Sr. He was most detestible and loathsome
in the eyes of Kate, for which no cause was ever
assigned. But the witch often declared its pur-
pose of killing him before leaving the place.
