Robertson county, who would please him, and he
could win her if he would stay; that she was
wealthy, possessing forty negroes and considera-
ble money. John laughed at the revelation as
supremely ridiculous, and left on the following
morning as contemplated, and was absent six
months or more, returning empty handed as
predicted. Very soon after his departure, the
young lady in question arrived, and left before
his return, and John never met her.
