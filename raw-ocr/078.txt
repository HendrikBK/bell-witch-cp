Phillis was a strong believer in “Ole Missus.”
Describing the incident she said: “I neber seed
Satan whipped outen er meetin’ so quick in aller
my bawn days. Sooner ‘an Ole Missus sot down
on dat man de devil tuek out vuder der flo an
de man hollered glory, glory, lemme up, lemme
up. Ole Miss paid no tention tu enybody. She
sot dar, an menced gittin happy hersef, an all de
foks in de house menced shoutin’. De man he
got so full uy glory he ware gwinter git up eny-
how an meneed drawing hiz hine legs up sorter
like er cow, an den drapped back, kase Ole Miss
ware still dar, an she want'er gwineter git up tell
ole Satan wuz mished clean outen him. Hit
made Mister Joe Edwards sweat like er hoss, but
he am got mighty good ligion now, dat will last
him tell der next meetin.”
