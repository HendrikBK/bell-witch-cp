Dear Str—Some years ago, while you were
engaged in publishing a newspaper at Springfield,
Tenn., Unele Joel Bell applied to me for the
manuscript of my father, Williams Bell, stating
that the application was made at your request for
the purpose of incorporating the same in a full
and complete history of the so-called Bell Witch,
which proposition I declined to accede to at that
time, for several reasons that need not now be
mentioned. However, one objection was, that
after writing his own memories, and the recollec-
tions of other members of the family, father con-

the surrounding circumstances, it was best that
