The witch told various stories concerning itself,
and said it could be anything, assume any form
it desired; a dog, a rabbit, bird, or human form.
It finally told the family that if Betsy would
marry a certain gentleman, it would leave and
not trouble them any more. The negroes could
tell the most wonderful stories, and narrate mir-
aculous escapes.
